4.2 Flow B: Complex Call with Escalation (xLink)

Trigger: Family member asks question xLink cannot answer
Actor: Family member with complex requirements
Goal: Capture information and arrange callback

Example Scenarios Requiring Escalation:
• Specific pricing questions ("How much for a mahogany casket?")
• Complex legal situations ("My brother and I disagree on arrangements")
• International repatriation questions
• Caller explicitly requests human
• Highly specific religious/cultural questions outside AI's training
Escalation Response:
"That's a really important question, and I want to make sure you get the most accurate answer. Let me arrange for Sarah, our funeral director, to call you back. She'll be able to help you with the specifics. What's the best number to reach you, and when would be a good time for her to call?"

System Actions:
• Captures callback number and preferred time
• Records the specific question asked
• Creates urgent callback task for director
• Includes call recording/transcript for context
4.3 Flow C: Director Conducts Meeting (xScribe)

Trigger: Family arrives for arrangement meeting
Actor: Funeral Director (Sarah)
Goal: Capture complete arrangement details, generate documentation

Pre-Meeting (Director):
18. Director opens xFunerals app on tablet/phone
19. Navigates to today's appointments
20. Reviews case summary (pre-populated from xLink call)
21. Notes key details: mother's name, son James is primary contact, Church of England
Meeting Start:
22. Director greets family, seats them in arrangement room
23. Director explains xScribe: "To make sure we capture everything accurately and don't miss any details, I use a recording assistant. This helps me focus completely on you rather than taking notes. Is that okay with you?"
24. Family gives verbal consent
25. Director taps "Start Recording" in app
26. App shows recording indicator and consent timestamp
During Meeting:
• Director conducts natural conversation
• xScribe records audio continuously
• Real-time transcription runs in background
• Director can glance at app to see key data being extracted
Meeting End:
27. Director taps "End Recording"
28. xScribe processes recording (15-30 seconds)
29. Pop-up appears: "Missing Information Checklist"
30. Director reviews with family: "Before we finish, let me just confirm a few details..."
31. Director verbally fills gaps (xScribe captures these too)
32. Director taps "Complete Meeting"
33. xScribe generates documents in background
Post-Meeting:
• Within 2 minutes: All documents available in dashboard
• Director reviews, makes minor edits if needed
• Sends contract to family for signature
• Total admin time: 5-10 minutes (vs. 4-6 hours manually)
